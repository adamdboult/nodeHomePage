0.1 Inner products

An inner product is a sesquilinear form with a positive-definite Hermitian matrix.

⟨u, v⟩=u*Hv

If we are using the real field this is the same as:

⟨u, v⟩=uTHv

Where H is now a symmetric real matrix.

0.1.1 Same

⟨v, v⟩=v*Hv

Always positive and real.
