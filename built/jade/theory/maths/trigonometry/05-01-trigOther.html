0.1 Other functions

0.1.1 Reciprocal trigonometric functions

Standard

$\csc (\theta ):=\frac{1}{\sin (\theta )}$

$\sec (\theta ):=\frac{1}{\cos (\theta )}$

$\cot (\theta ):=\frac{1}{\tan (\theta )}$

Hyperbolic

$csch (\theta ):=\frac{1}{\sinh (\theta )}$

$sech (\theta ):=\frac{1}{\cosh (\theta )}$

$coth (\theta ):=\frac{1}{\tanh (\theta )}$

0.1.2 Inverse trigonometric functions

Reciprocal standard

csc(arccsc(θ)) := θ

sec(arcsec(θ)) := θ

cot(arccot(θ)) := θ

Reciprocal hyperbolic

csch(arccsch(θ)) := θ

sech(arcsech(θ)) := θ

coth(arccoth(θ)) := θ
