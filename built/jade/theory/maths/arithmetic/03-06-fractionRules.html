Fraction rules

Addition

$\frac{A}{B}+\frac{C}{D}=\frac{AD+BC}{BD}$

Multiplication

$\frac{A}{B}\frac{C}{D}=\frac{AC}{BD}$

b Scaler addition

$C+\frac{A}{B}=\frac{BC+A}{B}$

Scaler multiplication

$C\frac{A}{B}=\frac{AC}{B}$

Other

$\frac{A+B}{C}=\frac{A}{C}+\frac{B}{C}$

$\frac{A}{B}=\frac{AC}{BC}$
