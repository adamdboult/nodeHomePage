0.1 Fraction rules

0.1.1 Addition

$\frac{A}{B}+\frac{C}{D}=\frac{AD+BC}{BD}$

0.1.2 Multiplication

$\frac{A}{B}\frac{C}{D}=\frac{AC}{BD}$

0.1.3 b Scaler addition

$C+\frac{A}{B}=\frac{BC+A}{B}$

0.1.4 Scaler multiplication

$C\frac{A}{B}=\frac{AC}{B}$

0.1.5 Other

$\frac{A+B}{C}=\frac{A}{C}+\frac{B}{C}$

$\frac{A}{B}=\frac{AC}{BC}$
